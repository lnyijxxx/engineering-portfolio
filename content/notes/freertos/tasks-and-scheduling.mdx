## Task priorities

Higher number = higher priority in FreeRTOS (opposite of some other RTOSes -
worth double-checking per port). A higher-priority task that never blocks
will starve every lower-priority task completely, not just slow them down.

## Stack sizing

Undersized task stacks corrupt adjacent memory silently rather than crashing
cleanly. Enable stack overflow checking (`configCHECK_FOR_STACK_OVERFLOW`)
during development - it's cheap insurance against a very hard-to-diagnose bug
class.
