## What it is

Controller Area Network - a differential two-wire bus (CAN_H/CAN_L) designed
for reliability in electrically noisy environments, originally automotive,
now common in robotics for inter-board communication.

## Termination

Both physical ends of the bus need a 120-ohm termination resistor. A bus with
only one end terminated will often "mostly work" at low message rates and
fail intermittently under load - which makes it a deceptively easy thing to
misdiagnose as a software timing issue instead of a hardware one.

## Arbitration

Lower CAN ID = higher priority. Nodes sending lower IDs win arbitration
automatically at the bit level with no collision, unlike protocols that need
a backoff/retry scheme.
