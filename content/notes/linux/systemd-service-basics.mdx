## Minimal unit file

A service that should survive reboots (like an MQTT broker on a Pi) needs
`WantedBy=multi-user.target` under `[Install]`, plus `Restart=on-failure`
under `[Service]` if you want automatic recovery from crashes rather than
staying down until a manual restart.

## Debugging

`journalctl -u <service-name> -f` for live logs is the first thing to reach
for over anything else when a service fails silently on boot.
