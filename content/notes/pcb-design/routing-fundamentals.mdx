## Crystal load capacitors

Load capacitor values should account for trace and pad parasitic
capacitance, not just the crystal's rated load capacitance. Use
`(CL - Cstray) x 2` per capacitor, where Cstray is typically 2-5pF for short
traces. Getting this wrong causes unreliable oscillator startup, which can
look like a completely dead board.

## Trace length matching

For crystal traces specifically: keep them short and symmetric (similar
length on both pins) to avoid unbalanced parasitic loading between the two
legs of the oscillator circuit.

## Ground plane on 2-layer boards

Without a dedicated ground layer, pour ground on both layers and stitch with
vias liberally near any high-frequency or high-current path, not just at the
board edges.
