---
title: "Nodes"
date: "2026-02-12"
tags: ["ros2"]
---

A node is a single-purpose process participating in a ROS2 graph - e.g. one
node reads a sensor, another plans a gait, another handles teleop input. The
Unix-philosophy analogy (many small programs, not one monolith) is
deliberate and generally worth following rather than fighting.
