title: "Flight-controller integration and calibration"
---

The airframe, motors, ESC, Pixhawk, GPS, receiver, and power hardware are
assembled. ArduPilot is installed and the system communicates with Mission
Planner. Accelerometer, compass, radio, flight modes, and safety parameters
have been configured.

ESC configuration and motor validation are still in progress. Flight testing
will follow the bench checks. Raspberry Pi companion-computer integration is a
future phase and has not yet been implemented.
