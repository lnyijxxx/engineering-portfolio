title: "Q1 2026 — build, ESC calibration hell, first hover"
---

Frame build went smoothly. The real time sink was ESC calibration: the motors
would arm but never respond to throttle input from the transmitter. Spent about
two days on this before finding the cause - the ESCs were in a firmware mode
that required a per-ESC calibration pass with the battery connected *before*
the flight controller, not after, which is the opposite of the usual order
suggested in most guides.

Once that was sorted, first hover in the garage (props off, obviously) showed
sane motor mixing. Next entry should cover the first real outdoor flight and
whatever [Mission Planner](/notes/mission-planner) throws at me during
telemetry setup.

Related: [UART notes](/notes/uart) were useful for debugging the telemetry
radio link during this stretch too - the radio uses UART under the hood and a
baud rate mismatch cost me an embarrassing hour.
