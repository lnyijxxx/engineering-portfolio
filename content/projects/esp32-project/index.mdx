## Overview

A custom embedded system designed as the electronics foundation for smart
safety glasses intended for electricians and field technicians. The PCB
integrates an ESP32-S3, OLED display, INMP441 digital microphone, Bluetooth
communication, and battery-powered operation.

## Completed scope

System architecture, component selection, schematic design, peripheral
integration, PCB routing, and manufacturing preparation were completed between
September and November 2025.

## Hardware architecture

- ESP32-S3-WROOM-1 processing module
- OLED display over I²C
- INMP441 digital MEMS microphone over I²S
- Bluetooth communication over UART
- Battery input and AMS1117 3.3 V regulation

## Status boundary

PCB design was completed. Physical fabrication, assembly, firmware development,
AI functionality, and device-level validation are not presented as completed.

## Engineering focus

Compact wearable PCB layout, multi-protocol peripheral integration, stable
power distribution, component selection, and manufacturing-aware design.
