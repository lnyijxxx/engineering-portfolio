title: "Platform architecture and hardware integration"
---

Defined the system architecture, selected the electrical components, and
integrated the Raspberry Pi 4 with eight LX-16A serial bus servos. The two-deck
layout separates compute from the battery and servo power electronics while
keeping wiring accessible for debugging.

Python servo communication and initial joint control are operational. The next
phase is focused on MATLAB inverse-kinematics simulation before coordinated
motion is deployed to the physical platform.
